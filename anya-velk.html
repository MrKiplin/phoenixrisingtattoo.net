Hi, I’m Anya Velk. I have been tattooing since 2014, and my creative path began in Siberia. Now, after 6 years, it continues in Toruń. It is a very interesting path and I enjoy it every day. I knew right away what style I would be working on, I love drawing graphite black and white images and that has been transferred to my tattoos. Sometimes I also want to experiment, I add color to the graphics and I really like the end result. I love unusual threads, laconic forms and clear lines. I see my paintings come to life on my skin and it really fascinates me. I find important to mention that almost all of my clients were very grateful to me that my needle was very light and did not cause much pain. I am waiting for your visit so that you can verify it personally!
